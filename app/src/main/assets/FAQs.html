Frequently Asked Questions

 

How does ‘The Box’ work?

‘The Box’ is an item-level-grocery-subscription service which is completely customizable. Choose what you want, how much you want, how soon you want them repeated and you are done forever!

‘The Box’ will ensure you’ll never have to:

·       Stand in those long boring queues at the supermarket

·       Go through the hassle of ordering the same things again and again

All items are neatly divided into various Boxes to ensure easy navigation throughout the application.

‘The Box’ offers a wide selection and ensures you ‘Never Run Out’ of your household groceries, fruits, vegetables, dairy products, personal care products, home care products, baby care products and pet care products

When do I pay?

The beauty about ‘The Box’ is that even though you have subscribed to products, you don’t have to pay anything upfront. You only have to pay for the items getting delivered and that too when they are getting delivered to you. No upfront payments. Period.

How do I pay?

Currently we only accept ‘Cash on Delivery’, you’ll be able to pay online very soon

Can I change, modify or delete my subscribed items?

Off course you can. Tap on ‘View Items’ in any of the Boxes in ‘My Boxes’. You’ll see a list of items you are subscribed to in that Box. You can change size, quantity or repeat rate whenever you want. If you want to unsubscribe to an item, you can do that too by making the quantity zero

Can I subscribe to more items anytime?

Yes. Select the item you wish to subscribe and merge it with any of the upcoming deliveries. The added item will get delivered to you with the delivery chosen by you and subsequently as per schedule

When will I get my first delivery?

Your first delivery will contain ALL your subscribed items based on the selected date and time when you checkout

How do I see my upcoming deliveries and its details?

Tap on ‘My Deliveries’ wherein you’ll find all the upcoming deliveries. Tap on any delivery to see what items are being delivered to you in that particular delivery

If any of my subscribed items hasn’t finished yet, can I delay the delivery for that item?

Yes. Tap on the delivery in which the item is scheduled. After you find the item, tap on ‘Adjust’. You’ll see the option of delaying the delivery

If any of my subscribed items has finished sooner than I expected, can I get it earlier?

Yes but only if you have atleast one delivery scheduled before the item in consideration is supposed to be delivered originally. Tap on the delivery in which the item is scheduled. After you find the item, tap on ‘Adjust’. You’ll see the option of getting it earlier, if available

Are there any delivery charges?

You’ll be charged Rs. 20 per delivery extra if your total monthly spending is less than Rs. 2000. Above Rs. 2000 all your deliveries will be free. We strongly recommend that you subscribe to a monthly spend of more than Rs. 2000

Can I return an item I received because it looks open, used or is delivered after the expiry date?

‘The Box’ has a no-questions-asked return policy. If we have delivered something that you are not satisfied with you can return it to our delivery executive at the time of delivery. You can deduct the price of that item from the payment you make to the delivery executive during delivery
